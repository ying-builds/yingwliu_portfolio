About

I grew up loving art in all forms — it was how I made sense of the world. But what actually shaped my instincts as a designer was growing up as an only child of immigrant Chinese parents. It taught me to read the room, pick up on what people aren't saying, and pay attention to whether something is actually working for them.

That's what I bring into my work: empathy that's practiced, not performed. I design by listening first — then turning what I hear into something clear and useful.

Outside of design, you'll find me creating content on TikTok, sharing learnings on Medium, or lifting at the gym. Balance keeps everything sharp.
